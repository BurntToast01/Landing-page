Lists:

You might find a need to show off a list. Why? Perhaps you want list the reasons for why you hate green eggs and ham.
The list could look like this:

  • green egs just don't look right
  • They are a crime against humanity
  • I'm vegan
  • It's 3AM, get those green eggs and ham away from me!


    • This looks fine, if you happen to enjoy bullet points. Let's say that the order matters to you, then it would look something like this:
    1. green egs just don't look right
    2. They are a crime against humanity
    3. I'm vegan
    4. It's 3AM, get those green eggs and ham away from me!


    Or, let's says you're an agent of chaos. The order of the list does not matter to you, bullet points are silly, and counting is for squares. Your list would look something like this:
      green egs just don't look right
      They are a crime against humanity
      I'm vegan
      It's 3AM, get those green eggs and ham away from me!
